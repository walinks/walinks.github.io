Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 30, 2026

At WALink Generator, we prioritize your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our WhatsApp Link Generator service.

1. Zero Data Collection

Our tool is designed to function entirely within your web browser. We do not store, collect, or share the phone numbers or messages you enter into our generator. All link and QR code generation happens client-side using JavaScript.

2. Local Storage

To provide a "Link History" feature, generated links are saved in your browser's localStorage. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers. You can clear this history at any time using the "Clear All" button in the History tab.

3. Third-Party Services

We use certain third-party scripts to enhance your experience:

  • Google Fonts: For typography.
  • QRCode.js: A library used locally in your browser to generate QR codes.
  • Cloudflare/GitHub Pages: For hosting and security.

4. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies or advertising pixels. Any cookies used by our hosting provider are strictly for technical performance and security purposes.

5. Contact Us

Since we do not collect personal data, we have no "user accounts" to manage. If you have questions regarding this policy, you can contact us via our GitHub repository.
